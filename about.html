About Our Department

The Department of Physics at DTU has been at the forefront of physics education and cutting-edge research for over eight decades. Our legacy of excellence has positioned us as leaders in scientific discovery and technological innovation.

We combine rigorous academic training with hands-on research opportunities, preparing students to tackle the most pressing challenges in physics and related disciplines.

Our Mission

To cultivate a vibrant intellectual community that pushes the boundaries of physical knowledge while training the next generation of scientists and innovators through:

World-Class Education

Innovative curriculum taught by renowned faculty with small class sizes for personalized attention.

Cutting-Edge Research

Interdisciplinary research programs with state-of-the-art facilities and equipment.

Technology Innovation

Translating fundamental discoveries into practical applications that benefit society.

Research Facilities

Our department houses world-class facilities that enable groundbreaking research across multiple physics disciplines:

  • ▶ Advanced Research Laboratories: 10,000 sq ft of specialized labs
  • ▶ High-Performance Computing Center: 5 petaflop supercomputer cluster
  • ▶ Nanotechnology Fabrication Unit: Class 100 cleanroom facilities
  • ▶ Quantum Research Center: Ultra-low vibration and EM-shielded labs
